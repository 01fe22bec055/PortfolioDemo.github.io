About Me

I’m Tanmay Akalwadi, a 3rd year Electronics & Communication Engineering student with a passion for AI, game development, and immersive interactive worlds. From a young age, I was fascinated by how games blend storytelling, creativity, and cutting-edge technology—and that passion has guided my journey both inside and outside the classroom.

Alongside building intelligent algorithms and applying machine learning to real gaming challenges, I have hands-on experience creating digital assets for games—working with tools like Blender, Unreal Engine and Unity to design models, textures, and environments. I’m driven to understand the entire pipeline: from crafting compelling assets to coding dynamic gameplay systems.

My technical toolkit includes Python, C, Java, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Unity, and Unreal Engine. Whether I’m designing smart NPCs, implementing pathfinding, analyzing player data, or developing unique game content, I always aim to combine technical precision with creativity. My ultimate goal is to help build the next generation of games that engage, inspire, and push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
